### Sublime Text
Go to line = `⌘ + G`  
Toggle Bookmark = `CTL + F2`  
Word Wrap = Shift +  `CTL + W`  
Distraction Free Mode = `CMD + CTL + SHIFT + F`  
Toggle Iterm = `⌘ + CTRL + T`
